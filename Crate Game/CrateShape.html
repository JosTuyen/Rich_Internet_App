Crate Board

Rules:
Push the target to the origin position of pusher.
Press: w :up ; d :right ; s :down ; a :left ; x :explode
One move or one turn costs 1 score
One explode costs 100 scores
The maximum weight which pusher can push is 3
The target symbol and weight:
A: 1 ; B: 2 ; C: 3
The direction of pusher:
^: North ; >: East ; v: South ; <: West
You can only push the wages on your direction
